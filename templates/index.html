Data Source

Why Our Dataset Is Reliable

SmartResist is powered by the NCBI Antimicrobial Resistance Portal — a gold-standard, peer-reviewed database maintained by the National Center for Biotechnology Information. The dataset links pathogen genomic markers (genotype) directly to observed drug resistance outcomes (phenotype).

The data spans 48 bacterial species, providing broad coverage of clinically significant pathogens, and is continuously curated by the global scientific community.
